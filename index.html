Our Story

More Than Just Food

Jucy's Ace is your go-to spot for hearty burgers, fresh shawarma, creamy shakes, and a full cocktail bar — all under one roof. Nestled in a lush, nature-surrounded setting, we've built a place where great food meets great company.

Whether you're grabbing a quick bite at our counter, chilling at the bar upstairs, or lounging with friends on our open-air terrace, there's a seat for you here. We're a local joint with big flavors and even bigger heart.

Upstairs Lounge

The Bar

Our open-air bar sits on the upper level with a stunning view of the surrounding forest. With a full lineup of spirits, cocktails, and beers — plus a beer tower for groups — this is the place to unwind after a long day.

Lit up with LED fairy lights against a sleek black marble counter, the bar is as beautiful as the drinks it serves.
