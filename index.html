Our Story

More Than Just Food

Jucy's Ace is your go-to spot for hearty burgers, fresh shawarma, creamy shakes, and a full cocktail bar — all under one roof. Located permanently at Bungan Bungan Falls, nestled in lush nature.

Whether you're grabbing a quick bite at our counter, chilling at the bar, or lounging on our open-air terrace — there's a seat for you here. A local joint with big flavors and bigger heart.
